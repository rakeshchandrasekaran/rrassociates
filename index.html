R. Ramalingam & Associates, Chartered Accountants, is a premier professional services firm established in 2004, headquartered in Chennai, India. We are committed to delivering excellence through a client-first approach across all our practice areas.

Our mission is to empower businesses with comprehensive financial strategies, ensuring compliance, growth, and long-term value creation. We serve corporates, LLPs, firms, individuals, NRIs, and foreign nationals across India.
